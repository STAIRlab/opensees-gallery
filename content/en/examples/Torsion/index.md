\[
\begin{array}{rll}
(\nabla \cdot \nabla) \varphi &=0 & \text { in } \Omega, \\
\nabla_{\mathbf{n}} \varphi  &=-\FrameBasis \times \PlanePosition \cdot \mathbf{n} & \text { on } \partial \Omega
\end{array}
\]

For this problem, `ndm=2` and `ndf=1`.
